Modern Slavery Policy

1. Purpose

This Modern Slavery Policy outlines the commitment of Greendome Global Education Pvt Ltd to preventing modern slavery and human trafficking in all aspects of our operations and supply chains. We are dedicated to ensuring that no one working with or for our company is exploited or subjected to forced labour.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, suppliers, service providers, and partners associated with Greendome Global Education Pvt Ltd. We expect all parties who work with us to comply with our values and demonstrate their commitment to eliminating modern slavery in their practices.

3. Our Commitment

At Greendome Global Education Pvt Ltd, we commit to:

  • Ensuring that no modern slavery, human trafficking, or forced labour occurs within our business operations or supply chains.
  • Conducting regular assessments to identify risks of modern slavery and implementing measures to mitigate these risks.
  • Fully complying with Indian laws and international regulations concerning modern slavery and human rights.
  • Fostering an environment of transparency, integrity, and ethical business conduct throughout our organisation and partners.

4. Due Diligence

To prevent modern slavery within our business, we follow these due diligence practices:

  • Evaluating and monitoring our supply chain and partners to ensure they meet our ethical standards.
  • Requiring suppliers and contractors to confirm their commitment to combating modern slavery through written agreements.
  • Performing regular audits and reviews of our supply chain to detect and eliminate any risks of modern slavery.

5. Training and Awareness

Greendome Global Education Pvt Ltd provides training and resources to all employees to raise awareness about modern slavery risks, including:

  • Educating staff on identifying signs of modern slavery and human trafficking.
  • Equipping them with the knowledge to take appropriate action when concerns arise.
  • Promoting a culture of vigilance and responsibility across our team and partners.

6. Reporting and Compliance

We encourage all employees, contractors, and stakeholders to report any concerns related to modern slavery. Reports can be made anonymously, and all information will be treated with confidentiality. Our approach includes:

  • Promptly investigating reports and ensuring the safety and protection of affected individuals.
  • Taking corrective actions, including terminating contracts with suppliers found to be involved in modern slavery.
  • Collaborating with relevant authorities to address any legal violations or misconduct.

7. Responsibility

The management of Greendome Global Education Pvt Ltd is responsible for implementing and monitoring this policy. All employees and business partners are required to comply with this policy, and non-compliance will result in appropriate disciplinary actions.

8. Review

This Modern Slavery Policy will be reviewed annually to ensure that it remains effective in combating modern slavery and protecting human rights within our operations and supply chains.
