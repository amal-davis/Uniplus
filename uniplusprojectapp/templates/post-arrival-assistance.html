Our relationship with students does not end once they board the flight. At Edroots, we provide post-arrival service to all students to make them comfortable in a foreign country. Our service includes, airport pick up assistance, accommodation assistance, insurance, setting up accounts, insurance, getting sim cards, and travel card arrangements. The students will get assistance from our alumni students who were currently studying in various universities. They help them to get acquainted with new lifestyle and culture. Those you complete their course will get Post-study work permit assistance and P.R guidance.
